Alimi Ibraheem becomes unilag overall best graduating student

"My parents and extended family thought i was going to study Medicine due to my smartness"

Posted by Unilag Blog on January 18, 2021

Overall UNILAG best graduating student, Alimi Ibraheem from Mechanical Engineering said, it was great to emerge as UNILAG best graduating student.

Ibraheem with CGPA 4.98 made it known that his parent wanted him to study medicine but could not because of his personal allergies.

"My parents and extended family thought i was going to study Medicine due to my smartness. I knew i couldn't cope with medicine due to my fear of blood, mutilated bodies, human parts, and all the situations doctors face. My next option was engineering because growing up, i loved cars and had a dream of building my own car and i also love Mathematics. so, Mechanical Engineeringfelt like the perfect chioce""

He also did not fail to tell us his experience as a student of unilag which includes the good and bad moments and how is course Mechanical Engineering was demanding at onset

My five years experience in the University had its moments, the good and bad ones. Mechanical Engineering itself was very demanding and stressful right from the onset. The rigorous and voluminous lab reports are an important highlight. I was able to make new friends and families, further extending my connections.
