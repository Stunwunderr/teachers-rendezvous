Ekeoseye Elisabeth Osereme emerges unilag overall best graduating student

Miss Osareme garnered a total of 4.95 CGPA

Posted by Unilag Blog on January 18, 2022

Ekeoseye Elisabeth Osereme, has emerged as the best graduating student from the University of Lagos for the 2019/2020 academic session.

Miss Osareme with Bsc. Environmental Toxicology and conservation option garnered a total of 4.95 CGPA at the 52nd 2020 c0nvocation ceremonies.
