UNILAG student dies.

he died at the school medical centre

Posted by Unilag Blog on February 20, 2022

University of Lagos lost a student (name undisclosed), he died at the school medical centre as a result of blocked urinary tract he had. The school medical centre tried their best to save him but later lost him.

His friends, coursemates and well wishers matched round the school to mourn him.

One of his friend who prefers to be anonymous said he was very jovial and fun to be with, everyone likes to be around him.
