Teachers’ Rendevouz

Teachers’ Rendezvous is an online forum for discussions supporting good teaching and teachers in Nigeria and Africa by extension.

TEACHERS’ RENDEZVOUS ONLINE MAGAZINE is an open forum for discussion(s) that supports good teaching and teachers in Nigeria and Africa by extension.

Teaching is a highly social profession which is strongly considered as the most significant in any human society. Hence, the need for an effective and vibrant platform that promotes teacher collaboration.

Being a teacher doesn’t mean you stop learning. One of the most important things you learn as a teacher is drawing support from one another.

The magazine is out to promote innovation in education and helps to play the important role of fostering a sense of community among Nigerian teachers from all strata.

It will avail every teacher the opportunity to have direct access to the knowledge, experience, and resources of countless educators whom they may have never connected with in their immediate professional circles.

The magazine is also aimed at bridging the current gap in teacher collaboration in Nigeria and to address the highest priority teaching and learning needs in Nigeria.
